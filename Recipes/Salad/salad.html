Greek Salad

This classic Greek salad recipe is full of fresh vegetables, feta cheese, and olives, and is dressed with a simple olive oil and vinegar dressing.

  • Ingredients:
  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1 large cucumber, chopped
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 4 ounces feta cheese, crumbled
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Caprese Salad

This simple Caprese salad recipe is made with fresh mozzarella, ripe tomatoes, and basil, and is drizzled with a balsamic glaze.

  • Ingredients:
  • 2 large ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Chicken Caesar Salad

This classic Caesar salad recipe is topped with grilled chicken and homemade croutons, and is dressed with a creamy Caesar dressing.

  • Ingredients:
  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 2 boneless, skinless, chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
